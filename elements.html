Autonomous Systems

This project offered several advantages for package delivery and wildfire monitoring using drones. I developed a quaternion mathematical model for drones in different frames and simulated it in MATLAB. I also created a novel quaternion library. Additionally, I proposed a novel control algorithm based on sliding mode. The project included programming, implementation, and derivation of the mathematical model for the quadrotor. The main goal of the project was to ensure accurate tracking and stability of the quadrotor. We achieved 100% accurate tracking even in the presence of uncertainties.

Publication: A. Shevidi and H. A. Hashim, “Adaptive Backstepping and Non-singular Sliding Mode Control for Quadcopter UAVs with Unknown Time-varying Uncertainties,” In Proc. of the 2024 IEEE American Control Conference (ACC’24), Toronto, Ontario, Canada, pp. 1-7, 2024.
