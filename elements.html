Autonomous Systems

This project provided multiple advantages to deliver packages and monitor wildfire by drones. I developed quaternion Mathematical Model of drones in different frame and simulated in MATLAB. A novel quaternion library was developed by myself. Then I proposed a novel control algorithm which was based on Sliding Mode. Progarmming, implementation and deriving mathematical model of quadrotor was conducted. The main goal of the project was to guarantee acurate tracking and Stability of quadrotor. 100% accurate tracking was guaranteed in the presence of uncertainties.

Publication: A. Shevidi and H. A. Hashim, “Adaptive Backstepping and Non-singular Sliding Mode Control for Quadcopter UAVs with Unknown Time-varying Uncertainties,” In Proc. of the 2024 IEEE American Control Conference (ACC’24), Toronto, Ontario, Canada, pp. 1-7, 2024.
